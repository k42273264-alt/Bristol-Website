Meetings & Events

Premier Event Spaces

Host your corporate meetings, conferences, or private events at Bristol Grand Hotel by Sunday. Our 11 versatile event spaces, accommodating 10 to 500 delegates, blend Victorian grandeur with modern facilities, perfectly located in Bristol’s historic Old City, just a short walk from Bristol Temple Meads Station.

Our Venues

Flexible Event Spaces

Wessex Suite

Spacious venue accommodating up to 500 delegates in theatre style, ideal for large conferences, banquets, and receptions.

Ballroom

Accommodates up to 300 theatre style, perfect for weddings, banquets, or mid-sized events.

Marlborough Suite

Versatile space for up to 100 theatre style, suitable for smaller conferences and private events.

Duchess Suite

Main suite with capacity for 120 theatre style, supported by five additional Duchess rooms for breakout sessions.

Devon

Flexible space hosting up to 60 theatre style, suitable for training sessions or workshops.

Brunel

Mid-sized venue for up to 30 theatre style, ideal for meetings and private gatherings.

Clifton

Intimate meeting room accommodating up to 10 delegates in theatre or boardroom style.
