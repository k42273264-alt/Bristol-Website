Festive Celebrations

A Magical Christmas in Bristol

Celebrate the festive season at Bristol Grand Hotel by Sunday, located in the historic Old City near Bristol Temple Meads Station. Our elegant Grand Ballroom and Thecha Restaurant provide the perfect setting for party nights, private gatherings, and Christmas Day lunch, with seasonal Asian-inspired menus, festive décor, and 186 rooms for a holiday escape.
