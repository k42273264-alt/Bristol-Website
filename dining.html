Our Restaurant

Its Thecha, our signature Asian restaurant, offers an all-day dining experience featuring breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Enjoy authentic Asian flavors with a modern twist in an elegant setting accommodating up to 60 guests at a time. Reservations are available, subject to availability.

  • Cuisine: Indian, Pan Asian and Traditioanl English
  • Breakfast Timings: Monday to Friday: 7:00 AM – 10:00 AM | Saturday & Sunday: 7:00 AM – 10:30 AM
  • Lunch & Dinner: Available throughout the day
  • Capacity: 60 guests seated

Bar & Lounge: Open daily from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
