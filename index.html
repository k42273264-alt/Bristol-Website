A Timeless Escape

Set in the heart of Bristol’s historic Old City, The Grand Hotel by Sunday is a Grade II listed Victorian masterpiece, blending heritage with modern luxury. With 186 stylish rooms, it’s the perfect base to explore Bristol’s vibrant culture, street art, and harbourside attractions.

Rooms & Suites

Elegant Retreats

Our 186 rooms, including Standard Doubles, Privilege Rooms with espresso machines, and luxurious Suites, offer Victorian charm with modern amenities like free Wi-Fi, ensuite bathrooms, and complimentary toiletries. Perfect for a stylish stay in Bristol.

Getting Here

Arrive Easily

Nearest station: Bristol Temple Meads – 0.9 miles walking, 1.2 miles driving

Bristol Bus and Coach Station – 0.3 miles walking

From M4/M5:

Exit at M32 towards Bristol city centre.

Follow signs to Old City and Broad Street.

Parking available in adjacent multi-storey car park (£19 per 24 hours).

11 miles to Bristol International Airport.
